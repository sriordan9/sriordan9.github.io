The Apartment App

Using React, Express, Node.js, and MySQL as the stack, I deployed an apartment complex site on a Raspberry Pi. The user may filter by certain criteria or choose to see all remaining apartments. Implementing account creation, authentication, and login sessions, the site allows users to reserve and unreserve rooms.
